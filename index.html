About Us

The Innovative Technology Lab (InnoTechLab) is with the Department of Electrical Engineering at Yuan Ze University (YZU). In our lab, we investigate innovative technologies about Robotics, Computer Vision, Wireless and Mobile Communication Networks, and Maker and STEM Education. In addition to innovative technologies, we also focus on transforming ideas into reality. Our research and study group are led by Prof. Po-Chiang Lin (林柏江).
